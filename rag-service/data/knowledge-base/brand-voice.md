# CreatorOS Brand Voice Guide

## Overview
This document defines the voice, tone, and style for all AI-generated content. Use this as the foundational context for maintaining consistency across videos, scripts, and descriptions.

## Voice Characteristics

### Tone
- **Conversational**: Write like you're explaining to a curious friend
- **Authentic**: Avoid corporate jargon or overly formal language
- **Energetic**: Maintain enthusiasm without being over-the-top
- **Relatable**: Use everyday examples and scenarios

### Language Style
- **Hinglish-friendly**: Mix Hindi and English naturally where appropriate
- **Simple vocabulary**: Avoid complex words when simple ones work
- **Active voice**: Keep sentences direct and engaging
- **Short sentences**: Break down complex ideas into digestible parts

## Content Guidelines

### Hook Formula
1. Start with a provocative question or bold statement
2. Create curiosity gap within first 5 seconds
3. Promise clear value upfront

### Script Structure
1. **Hook** (0-10 seconds): Grab attention immediately
2. **Problem** (10-30 seconds): Define what the viewer struggles with
3. **Solution** (30-180 seconds): Deliver the core content
4. **Call to Action** (Last 10 seconds): Clear next step

### Avoid
- Clickbait without substance
- Overly promotional language
- Passive voice where active works better
- Filler words (basically, actually, literally)
- Generic advice without specific examples

## Target Audience
- Age: 18-35
- Interests: Technology, productivity, career growth
- Pain points: Information overload, decision paralysis, time management
- Goals: Learn faster, work smarter, stay ahead

## Formatting Notes
- Use bullet points for lists
- Include timestamps for longer content
- Break scripts into clear sections
- Add visual cues for B-roll moments
